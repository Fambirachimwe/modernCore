Experience Westminster

We are an independent school with small classes, high expectations and a committed staff team. Each pupil is valued as a unique and gifted individual. Pupils achieve highly in their results and co-curricular activities with the School’s full support and encouragement. Located a few minutes’ walk from Borrowdale Road and Harare Drive Junction the school is accessible from a wide area of Zimbabwe and the surrounding countries.
